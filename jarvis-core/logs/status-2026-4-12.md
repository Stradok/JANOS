### Architecture
- **LLM**: qwen2.5:7b-instruct (via Ollama)
- **Fallback**: Keyword router for when LLM drops JSON
- **Voice**: edge-tts → ffplay (MP3)
- **Audio Input**: sounddevice → WAV → STT
- **Web**: requests + regex (no Playwright/Selenium needed)
- **Apps**: subprocess.Popen + os.startfile
